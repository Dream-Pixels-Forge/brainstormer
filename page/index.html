See how it works
01 — Input

Spark

Speak your idea or type it in. Real-time voice transcription captures your thoughts as they come — no formatting, no friction. Hit Spark and the AI breaks it down.

02 — Explore

Brainstorm

The AI asks one question at a time — building deep context as you answer. Configurable depth, research mode, and voice I/O keep the session flowing naturally.

03 — Deliver

Export

From your Q&A session, Brainstormer generates 8 project files: idea brief, tasks, roadmap, architecture, design decisions, agent configs, and more. Ready to drop into any project.

04 — Native

Desktop Native

Built with Tauri 2 — a lightweight, cross-platform shell. Frameless window, system tray, global shortcut. It stays out of your way until you need it. Ctrl+Shift+B toggles it from anywhere.
